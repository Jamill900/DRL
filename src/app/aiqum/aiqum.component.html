Challenge

The precipitation and deposition of solids are a major challenge in the production of oil and gas. Flow assurance solids are formed because of unavoidable changes in temperature, pressure, and composition of the oil-gas-water flowstream, from reservoir conditions to processing conditions. The advent of subsea production and the increased exploitation of heavy crudes have made flow assurance issues dominant in ensuring efficient and safe exploitation of hydrocarbon assets.

AIQUM (standing for "AI" as artificial intelligence and "QUM" as "Sand" in Azerbaijani language) is a breakthrough surveillance system approach using real-time high frequency working technology, providing sand production alerts every millisecond. Using advanced ultrasonic and fiber-optic (i.e., Distributed Acoustic Sensing - DAS) sensors with AI algorithms, it provides operators with real-time data for solid production identification and quantification.

Our Solution

This technology has been developed on a flow loop facility since the beginning of the project. The real-time outcome of this product installed on BP AGT's assets will step up safe operations and boost well production expectancy. Technology is scalable to any asset where solid production becomes a bottleneck for operation efficiency.
